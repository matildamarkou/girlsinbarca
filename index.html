🌸 Girls Trip: Barcelona + Costa Brava 🌸

Fun, colorful, Pinterest-perfect 4-day itinerary!

✨ Thursday: Arrival & Exploring ✨

🌸 Les Filles Café

Healthy garden oasis for brunch. 🌿🍳

🏰 Sagrada Família

Gaudí's masterpiece. Epic photo ops inside with the stained glass! 📸

🛍️ Passeig de Gràcia

Shopping at Zara flagship, Loewe, Chanel! Stop by Casa Batlló! 🏠✨

🌮 Lunch at Bastaix

Rustic tapas and wine spot in El Born. 🍷🍤

🍷 Dinner at Gresca

Modern Catalan food, natural wines, minimalist vibes! ✨

🍸 Drinks at Boca Chica

Glamorous cocktail night to kick off the trip! 🎉

💖 Friday: Culture, Food & Night Out 💖

🥐 Breakfast at Lady Babka

Croissants, pastries, and cute coffee moments. ☕🍰

🏛️ Gothic Quarter

Wander the historic streets, cute photos around every corner! 📸

🍷 Lunch at Bar Alegría

Classic tapas in a historic building — very "old Barcelona" vibe. 🇪🇸

🖼️ Picasso Museum

Soak up the art vibes and get inspired! 🎨

🍸 Dinner & Night at Boca Grande & Chica Club

Seafood dinner then glam cocktail bar and dancing! 🥂💃

🌊 Saturday: Costa Brava Road Trip 🌊

🚗 Drive to Tossa de Mar

Charming seaside town with medieval castle views! 🏰🌊

🏖️ Swim at Calella de Palafrugell

Picture-perfect beach town. Bring swimsuits and cute hats! 👙👒

🍝 Lunch by the beach

Fresh seafood with your toes in the sand. 🦐🍷

🌅 Sunset Drive Back

Playlist blasting, windows down, sunset photos. 🎶🚗

🍳 Sunday: Chill Vibes & Goodbye Barcelona 🍳

🥞 Brunch at Picnic

Best brunch spot for a final treat! 🥂🍓

🏞️ Park Güell

Colorful mosaics and city views — must-take photos! 🌸🏰

🍽️ Farewell Dinner at Terraza Martínez

Terrace dinner with views over Barcelona — perfect goodbye. 🌇🍷
